Artist Statement

CNK Cyborg, Digital Image, 2019

CNK Cyborg is a collaborative digital image. It was composed by three artists and was created individually and then passed onto the next artist. Each artist was able to add whatever they wanted onto the image before passing it onto the next.

An Afternoon at the Library, Digital Image, 2019

The concept for this collaborative piece was to create a composite image that has to do with a place. The size of our image is what is unifying , 11x14. I devided to work from an image that I took myself at the Huntington Library in Los Angeles. I wanted to create a hyper reality that would take place at the library on a Sunday afternoon. I wanted to blend the fine art style that the library has and the community that surrounds LA. I want it to be a relaxed fun afternoon that turns into an outdoor party. I want the viewer to have to take their time and reallt look at the small details of the image rather than it be something that is obvious.

Nostalgic Dreams, Audio/Video, 2019

The concept for Nostalgic Dreams was about coming to terms with my OCD tendencies. I tend to replay moments in my mind constantly throughout my days and this video.audio I created represent these thoughts and emotions.

Queer Pool, Net Art, 2019

This collaborative piece's concept was inspired by the Yahoo Pool game of the early 2000s. We customized it to make it relevant to our identities and community. We envisioned Queer Pool a place where friendships could be built. It serves as one of our early internet memories and a fictional chatroom.

Luna y Sol

Luna y Sol, Code Art, 2019

Luna y Sol is a collaborative piece between Carla and myself. The concept for this piece was to incorporate movement and interaction between the moon and sun over two different backgrounds.

Untitled 1, 2, 3, GIF, 2019

This series are gifs with incorporated glitches. This work creates disruption in the movement and repetition of light. The work is meant to create distortion in commonly known arcade games which gives the viewers a skewed view of the subjects.
