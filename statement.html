Artist Statement

My work focuses on capturing the authenticity of my subjects through my experiences as a queer, latinx, first generation born individual. Through my work, I am exploring my identity, culture, and feelings of displacement in the United States since my roots are from Mexico. Although my work that I am presenting are digital images, I prefer working in analog. I work with a medium format camera and digitally scan my negatives. I am currently focusing on improving my digital camera skills and digital editing. My future goals consist of traveling, documenting, and finding my communities where I feel “at home.” I am striving to be able to show my work in galleries.
